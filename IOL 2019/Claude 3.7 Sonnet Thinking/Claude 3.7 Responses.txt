Problem 5: Nooni Language Analysis
Part (a): Translating Nooni to English

bvúsɔ̀w nǔ fɔ̀ shě ntfǔː.
"Bvusɔw is the day after tomorrow."
me nú ŋgéː nyɛ́n kèŋkfǔ lé ɛ̀bɛ̀n.
"I am about to steal the yam just now."
wvù tɔ̀ nɔ̂ː yɛ᷅n bèsèn ě bvúmbòn.
"He saw us on Bvumbon."
bèsèn nǔ bóy tɔ́ fɔ̀wǎy bvúzhı᷇ːde᷆n.
"We will help to go to the market on Bvuzhiden."

Part (b): The order of Nooni days
The order of the Nooni week (starting with Bvutfu) is:

Bvutfu
Bvumbon
Bvusɔw
Bvukema
Bvuŋka
Bvuŋkaden
Bvuzhiden

Part (c): Translating English to Nooni

On Bvusɔw, I helped the man.
"me cǐ nɔ̂ː fí dìɛ̀msɛ̌n bvúsɔ̀w."
The thief stole the yam just now.
"cɔ̌ŋ géː kèŋkfǔ lé ɛ̀bɛ̀n."
On Bvuŋkaden, I will hear the car.
"me nú ŋwóy wengambɔŋ mutù bvûŋka᷆ːde᷆n."
Today, the woman will kill the man.
"kwɔ᷆ːn nú yó dìɛ̀msɛ̌n ɛ̀bɛ̀n."

"dìɛ̀msɛ̌n mbɛ̂ː yɛ᷅n wɔ̀ ɛ̀bɛ̀n."
