Problem 1: Yonggom

**(a) Translate into English**

*   Om benu aneen.
    *   Translation: She takes the bread and he eats it.
*   Munuuna wunuub.
    *   Translation: He comes and we will go away.
*   Wingkiiwa wengamburuun.
    *   Translation: You will sing and hear.
*   Anon ye weng wengamberenib yeedaraniib.
    *   Translation: They hear the dog's voice and they will get up.
*   Oon wedmaniina karub wedmaneeb.
    *   Translation: I will see the fish and you will see the man.
*   Ok wedmeena aniin.
    *   Translation: He saw the water and I will drink it.

**(b) Translate into Yonggom**

*   We will go away and they will come.
    *   Yonggom: Wenenub minuub.
*   I take the pig and he takes the fish.
    *   Yonggom: Awon baina oon baneen.
*   He saw the water and the house and heard the dog.
    *   Yonggom: Ok awon wedmoona anon wengambaraneen.
*   You(sg) eat the bread and sing.
    *   Yonggom: Om aniiwa wingkeewa.

Problem 5: Nooni

**(a) Translate into English (spoken on Bvuŋkaden - Day 5)**

*   bvúsɔ̀w nǔ fɔ̀ shě ntfǔː.
    *   Translation: Bvusɔw is the day after tomorrow.
*   me nú ŋgéː nyɛ́n kèŋkfǔ lé ɛ̀bɛ̀n.
    *   Translation: I am about to steal the yam just now.
*   wvù tɔ̀ nɔ̂ː yɛ᷅n bèsèn ě bvúmbòn.
    *   Translation: He found us on Bvumbon.
*   bèsèn nǔ bóy tɔ́ fɔ̀wǎy bvúzhı᷇ːde᷆n.
    *   Translation: We will find the market on Bvuzhiden.

**(b) The first day of the Nooni week is Bvutfu. What is the order of the Nooni days?**

1.  Bvutfu
2.  Bvusɔw
3.  Bvukema
4.  Bvuŋka
5.  Bvuŋkaden
6.  Bvumbon
7.  Bvuzhi

**(c) Translate into Nooni (spoken on Bvumbon - Day 7)**

*   On Bvusɔw, I helped the man.
    *   Nooni: me nɔ̂ː ncáw dìɛ̀msɛ̌n bvúsɔ̀w.
*   The thief stole the yam just now.
    *   Nooni: cɔ́ŋ nú kèŋkfǔ fɔ̀wǎy lé.
*   On Bvuŋkaden, I will hear the car.
    *   Nooni: me nú ŋgéː mutù bvûŋka᷆ːde᷆n.
*   Today, the woman will kill the man.
    *   Nooni: kwɔ᷆ːn nú géː dìɛ̀msɛ̌n ɛ̀bɛ̀n.
*   Today, the man saw you(sg).
    *   Nooni: dìɛ̀msɛ̌n nú yɛ́n wɔ̀ lé ɛ̀bɛ̀n.
