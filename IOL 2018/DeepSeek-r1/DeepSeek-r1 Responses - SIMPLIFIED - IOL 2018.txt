**(b) Portuguese borrowings**

*   **Difference from Native Words:** Native Terêna words exhibit initial consonant mutation (e.g., `mb` → `p`). Portuguese borrowings show medial consonant mutation instead (e.g., "lámbina" → "leápina" where medial "mb" mutates to "p", but initial "l" remains unchanged). Vowel changes also occur (e.g., `á` → `eá`).
*   My tin can: lámbatana
*   My cloak: kámbapana
