Problem 1 Solutions

(a) Numerals in digits:

	•	moeto waroewo — 120
	•	ijema joka rati — 80 (This has the same value as mepina daemita moeto)
	•	moeto wido ma benomi ka wi — 343 (This has the same value as moeto idibi ma widoma mepija ka benomi)

(b) Write out in Ekari:

	1.	1: Cannot be determined from the given data.
	2.	19: ije joka rati
	3.	26: joka rati ma wija ka ije
	4.	104: (mepija ka ije) daemita moeto (or (wija joka rati ka ije) daemita moeto)
	5.	292: moeto ije ma mepija ka ije ka wido ka benomi
	•	This number can be expressed in two ways: 19 (benomima rati ma benomi)

Problem 4 Solutions

(a) Exception and corrected form:

	•	Exception: wẽ́nẽ́ -> tṹwẽtіnẽ; Expected form: tẽ́wẽ́tẽ́nẽ́ (following pattern of CVCV words like wẽr̀ũ̀ -> tũ̀wẽt̀ũ̀rũ̀, where tV prefix and tV infix copy the vowel quality of the syllable they attach to/precede).

(b) Agbirigba forms:

	1.	rʊ́tʊ̂ — tʊ́rʊ́tʊ́tʊ̂
	2.	rɛẁʊ̃́ — tɛ́rɛ́tʊ̃́wʊ̃́ (or tɪ́rɛ́tʊ̃́wʊ̃́ depending on prefix vowel choice for ɛ)
	3.	bіḱó — túbіtókó (following bіśі ́ -> túbіt́іśі ́ for prefix)
	4.	ɲ́yẽ́ rɪỳã̀ — ɲ́tɪ̃́yẽ́ tɪ́rɪtã̀yã̀ (ɲ́yẽ́ as monosyllabic CV like ńdá, rɪỳã̀ as CVCV)
	5.	bɛḱʷʊ̃́ — tɛ́bɛ́tʊ̃́kʷʊ̃́
	6.	rіýõ̀rõ̀ — tírìtõ̀yõ̀tõ̀rõ̀ (adjusting tone on ri based on observed patterns)
	7.	hʷɔg̀â — tɔ́hʷɔtàgâ
